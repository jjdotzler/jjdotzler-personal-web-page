About Jack

Jack Dotzler is a senior at the University of Iowa, majoring in finance and business analytics. He also plays on the Iowa Hawkeye football team. Jack is #71 and plays offensive line. He is from Waunakee, Wisconsin where he grew up his entire life. His family includes his two parents, Katie and Jeff, and his two sisters, Anne and Ellen.

Jack has been playing football since he was 5 years old. He has always loved the game and is grateful to have the opportunity to play at the collegiate level. In his free time, Jack enjoys playing golf, reading books, and eating food. He is also a big fan of music and has a wide variety of favorite songs.

Jack's "why" is his Catholic faith. Ever since coming to college, he has been called to deepen his faith in the lord and live the life he was made for. Through the Newman Catholic Student Center on campus, Jack has found a thriving community of faithful christans to grow in faith with.
